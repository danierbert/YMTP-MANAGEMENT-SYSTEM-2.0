📜 Rules and Regulations

As part of the Youth Movement Through Progress (YMTP), all members are expected to follow these rules and regulations:

  1. Respect all fellow members, officers, and staff at all times.
  2. Attend all general assemblies, activities, and events unless with valid excuse.
  3. Submit all requirements on or before the given deadline.
  4. Promote discipline, honesty, and integrity in all undertakings.
  5. Properly use and care for the organization’s resources and facilities.
  6. Observe punctuality in every activity and meeting.
  7. Avoid any form of misconduct, violation, or abuse of authority.
  8. Follow the chain of command and organizational hierarchy.
  9. Support the organization’s mission, vision, and objectives.
  10. Non-compliance with the above rules may result in disciplinary action.
